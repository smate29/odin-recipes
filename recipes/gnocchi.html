Gnocchi


Description

Gnocchi is simple to make with just three ingredients: mashed potato, flour, and egg. This recipe is one my family has used for generations.

This gnocchi is "so tender and pillowy," says Allrecipes recipe tester Nicole Hopper. "Store-bought gnocchi can't even compare."


Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the potatoes, then mash or pass through a ricer.
  2. Combine the ingredients, then knead into a ball.
  3. Divide the dough into four even portions and shape into "snakes."
  4. Cut the snakes into pieces.
  5. Boil and drain the gnocchi.
